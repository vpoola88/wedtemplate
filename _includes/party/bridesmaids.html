BEST MAN

Kevin Biberacher


Kevin is one of my best friends that I met and lived with during my college and post college years. We initially met when he became “guy who is crashing on our couch for a while”, which shortly transformed to our roommate and good friend. We lived together for a cumulative total of over 6 years and I just can’t say enough good things about the guy. He is always fun to hang out with and we have enjoyed many good times together whether it’s an out of the country surf trip, an Iron Maiden or Danzig show, or just getting together for a couple beers and some laughs. I am honored that he will be up there for me on the big day.
